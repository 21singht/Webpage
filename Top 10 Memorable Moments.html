10 Most Memorable Moments

There have been many memorable moments in the history of soccer, but these 10 moments stand out the most.

    Arsene Wenger stealing the thunder from Sir Alex, winning the double in 1998. Van Persie's header against Spain in 2014.

  • Diana Ross missing a penalty in the World Cup opening ceremony in 1994. A sad day for all Jimmy Choo fans.
  • Germany, beaten earlier in the 1954 World Cup, 8-3 by Hungary, stun everyone to beat the same opponents 3-2 in the Final.
  • Maradona on his own responsible for crowning Napoli Serie A Champions in 1987.
  • Beckenbauer's chilling, restrained quip at English referee Jack Taylor in the World Cup 1974 final, moments after the ref has given Holland a penalty in the 2nd minute : "Taylor, you are an Englishman."
  • Steaua Bucuresti goalkeeper Helmuth Duckadam saving all 4 spaniard penalties against Barcelona in the 1986 European Cup final.
  • Liverpool's 5-4 win in the UEFA Cup in 2001 against Alaves.
  • Invincibles...Perugia finish unbeaten in 1979 in the Serie A, but end as runners up.
  • Germany beats Brazil 7-1
  • Brazil beats Germany to win a gold medal in the Olympics.
