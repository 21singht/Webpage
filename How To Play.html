How To Play

2 teams on one field, they have a coin toss and the team that wins decides if thet get the ball first or choose the side they start on.

Rules and Positions

  • Offsides-is if he or she is nearer to his opponents' goal line than both the ball and the second last opponent.
  • Goalkeeper-Is last person between the ball and the net. He is the only person on the field who can use his hands to stop the ball from going in his net or pick it up and punt it away.
  • Defender-The defender is a person who stays back on his team to stop the other team from getting to the goalkeeper or goal.
  • Midfield- is a mixture of defense and offense. A midfielder must have a lot of stamina, because its main job is to help defense and offense.
  • Offense-the position that scores most of the teams goals and stays closer to the the other team's goal and does not help that much in defense.
  • Fouls-a foul can be given if a person who is not the goaly touches the ball with his hand or when the slide tackle comes in contact with the person instead of the ball and if someone flops or gets in a fight.
